About Me

My Passions and Personality

I live in Barton-Under-Needwood, a coutryside village in the West Midlands. I have a love for Sports and Travel. I am a retired semi-professional snowboarder and national level gymnasts and have lived and worked in various countries around the world for ten years. I manage two property lets and have developed three properties.

My Life in Pictures

Computer Science

My dad is a retired Systems Engineer and I have grown up surrounded by the latest technology. After retiring from snowboarding, I built a website for my partner's carpentry business, this was the beginning of my interest in computer Science. The realisation of the sheer scope of opportunity, creativity and learning available has left me inspired to pursue a career in Software Development.
